6.15. arnettf

These fonts are chinese Unicode fonts which include the following charsets: Big5, GB2312-80, ISO8859-1,2,3,4,7,9,10,13,14,15, Bopomofo Extended for Minnan and Hakka.

HKSCS, CNS11643, GB18030, Japanese and Korean are work in progress.
